Project Assignment A

Project Overview

This website will be designed for a teacher, specifically Mrs. Yadon to store various parts of her classroom. The intended user of the website will be parents to stay up to date with information going on in their child’s class and also the children in Mrs. Yadon’s classroom. The website will have a navigation bar for various subjects such as Art, Music, PE, Math, etc., a list of classroom rules, contact information, an about me, worksheets, and possibly a calendar for the daily schedule of the students.

Page Design

Mrs. Yadon’s Classroom/Homepage

Name: “Mrs. Yadon’s Classroom”

Purpose: Introduce users to the website and welcome them

Users: Parents and possibly students

Content: Page will show an image of Mrs. Yadon and welcome you to the page. It wont have any data entry or validation. The page will have a navigation bar to go to different sections of the website

About me/Navigation Bar

Name: “About Me”

Purpose: This page will give the user a description and image of who Mrs. Yadon is

Users: The users will primarily be the parents of the students of Mrs. Yadon

Content: This page will not ask for user data. It will not need validation. It may contain buttons or hyperlinks. It will mainly be a description of the teacher

Contact Information/ About Me Subpage

Name: “Contact Information”

Purpose: List the information of the course the child is taking

Users: Primarily parents of the students of Mrs. Yadon

Content: This page can possibly ask for information. Possibly ask for name, email, and name of child, to be sent a newsletter. The data fields will need validation for email. The page will contain buttons to the navigation tab, will be able to navigate to other pages. It will contain information about Mrs. Yadon, how to get in contact with her

Classroom Rules/Navigation Bar

Name: “Classroom Rules”

Purpose: List the rules of the classroom of Mrs. Yadon

Users: Primarily the parents of the students of Mrs. Yadon

Content: This page will be a list of the rules implemented in Mrs. Yadons classroom such as raise your hand to speak, so parents are aware of the rules their child needs to follow. There will not be data entry needed on this page. It will show the navigation bar at the top

Calendar/Navigation Bar

Name: “Calendar”

Purpose: Keep parents up to date on things going on in the classroom

Users: Parents of the students of Mrs. Yadon

Content: This page will display the calendar of the schedule of students, and all other sub calendars combined such as daily calendar and snack calendar. Daily Calendar/Calendar Subpage

Purpose: Keep parents up to date on what their child will be doing in class that day

Users: Parents of the students of Mrs. Yadon

A sample format might be 8am – class starts, 8-9 science, etc. This page does not ask for user data and does not need validation. It might have buttons so when you click on a subject it will take you to that page for the resources instead of getting their from the resource tab
