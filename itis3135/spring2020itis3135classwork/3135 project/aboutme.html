About Me




Hi! I am Tiffany Yadon, also known to your kiddos as Mrs. Yadon.

I graduated college in 2018 at North Carolina State University. After graduating I moved to San Jose, California where I taught first grade in a private school for a year.

This year I am currently in Germantown, Maryland teaching first grade with your children at the lovely Fox Chapel Elementary School.

When I am not in school teaching your kiddos, I am attending school myself. I am currently seeking higher education and attending the online masters program for teachers at Clemson University.

I have been married almost three years, I have two dogs, and I am currently expecting!
